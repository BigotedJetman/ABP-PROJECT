¿What is the Radio?

When we talk about the Radio, we talk about a device that sends energy throug magnetic weaves. This do so we don't have cables. The device that seds the wave is known as Transmitter. The wave will travel from a pont A to point B until you reach to Receptor. This magnetic wave is captured by the radio antenna, transmitting to us the signal emitting. The circuit that there is inside the receiver is responsible for selecting the station that we tuned.
The radio waves they travel bye the air and have a series of features.

Speed: The time it takes for the wave to travel the path from Transmitter to the Receiver.

Wavelength: This is the distance between one wave peak and the next one.

Frecency: It is the number of waves that reach us and they measure themselves in Hertz.
